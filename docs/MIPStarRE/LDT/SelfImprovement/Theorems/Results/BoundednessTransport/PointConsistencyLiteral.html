    Natural-error point-consistency transport under monotone total overlap.

    If the projective replacement has no larger right-register total overlap with the point measurement than the helper submeasurement, then the submeasurement triangle argument is at the paper-natural error threshold selfImprovementHelperError + sqrt selfImprovementDataProcessingError.
