    Off-diagonal residual estimates for the helper SSC argument #

    This module names the off-diagonal scalar quantities appearing after the projector insertion in the helper strong-self-consistency proof, records their exact decompositions, and assembles the two variance-transport comparisons with the Schwartz--Zippel endpoint.

                    The first hypothesis is the variance replacement A^u_{h(u)} → A^v_{h(v)} in eq:swap-u-for-v-attack-of-the-clones.
