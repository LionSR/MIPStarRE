    Paper alignment #

    The main theorem switchSandwich (in Completeness.lean) assembles the paper's prop:switch-sandwich:

    E_x Σ_a ⟨ψ| A_a B A_a ⊗ I |ψ⟩
  ≈_{2√δ} E_x Σ_a ⟨ψ| B ⊗ A_a |ψ⟩
  ≈_{√δ} E_x Σ_a ⟨ψ| B A_a ⊗ I |ψ⟩

    where:

    • leftSandwichExpectation = leftTensor(A_a) * leftTensor(B) * leftTensor(A_a) = A_a B A_a ⊗ I
    • middleSandwichExpectation = leftTensor(B) * rightTensor(A_a) = B ⊗ A_a
    • rightSandwichExpectation = leftTensor(B * A_a) = B A_a ⊗ I

    The hypothesis BipartiteSDDRel ψ 𝒟 (IdxProjSubMeas.toIdxSubMeas A) (IdxProjSubMeas.toIdxSubMeas A) δ encodes A_a^x ⊗ I ≈_δ I ⊗ A_a^x — exactly the paper's eq:Aapproxd.
