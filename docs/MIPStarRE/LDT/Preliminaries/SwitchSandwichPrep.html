    Paper alignment #

    All definitions in this layer use the bipartite tensor formulation (leftTensor / rightTensor) matching the paper's prop:switch-sandwich and prop:cons-sub-meas:

    • diagonalSandwichFamily A B = A_a ⊗ B_a (paper's middle term for cons-sub-meas)
    • totalSandwichFamily A B = A_total ⊗ B_a (paper's right term)
    • Cauchy–Schwarz lemmas leftTensor_opBounded01, opBounded01_hermitian, etc. provide the operator-domain infrastructure for the switch-sandwich gap bounds.
