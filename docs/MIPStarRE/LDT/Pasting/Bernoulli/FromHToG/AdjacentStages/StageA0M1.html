    Section 12 pasting: from-H-to-G adjacent-stage source scalars #

    Definitions for the paper's adjacent-stage source scalars A₀ and M₁, together with their shape rewrites and the supporting move-right and half-sandwich context algebra lemmas.

    This file covers the definitions up to the first move-right step and the half-sandwich context algebra. The downstream chain M₁ → M₂ → M₃ → M₄ lives in AdjacentStages.Chain.
