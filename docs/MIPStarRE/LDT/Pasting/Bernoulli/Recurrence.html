    lem:from-H-to-G.

    The proof of the paper's Bernoulli-recurrence lemma uses exactly the two named upstream ingredients cited in the blueprint: cor:G-hat-facts for the \sqrt{2ζ} moves of \widehat G across the tensor factors, and lem:commute-g-half-sandwich for every suffix length appearing in the two \sqrt{ν₄} commutation moves. The conclusion package records the displayed scalar expectation inequalities from the paper, rather than a stronger ≈_δ statement between the already-averaged recurrence families. The scalar side conditions are threaded explicitly because the paper's absorption line for γ^(1/32) and ζ^(1/32) assumes these are normalized error parameters.
