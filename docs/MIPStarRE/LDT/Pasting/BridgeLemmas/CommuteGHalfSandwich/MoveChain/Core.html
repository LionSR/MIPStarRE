    Bridge: the staged move-commute-move chain for commuteGHalfSandwich.

    Constructs the sequence of 3k - 4 intermediate bipartite operator families joined by 3k - 5 elementary edges. These edges repeatedly move Ĝ₁ through the product Ĝ₁ · Ĝ₂ · ⋯ · Ĝₖ using self-consistency (move to right tensor, error 2ζ) and pairwise commutation (swap past neighbor, error ν₃), then compose them in one call to sddOpRel_chain, avoiding the exponential loss from recursive macro-chain composition.
