    Core role-register packages and predecessor-transport helpers used by the mainFormal assembly.

    Type (max u_1 u_2)

    Section 6 role-register induction output used by the mainFormal assembly.

    The main-induction call is applied to strategy.strategySymmetrization, whose local Hilbert space is indexed by Role × ι. This structure records exactly the piece of that call needed by the later unsymmetrization step: a polynomial POVM on the role register together with its symmetrized point-consistency estimate at the cascade scalar σ. It deliberately does not assert the factor-two unsymmetrized estimates; those remain the separate content of UnsymmetrizationBridgePackage.

      The role-register polynomial POVM produced by Section 6.
