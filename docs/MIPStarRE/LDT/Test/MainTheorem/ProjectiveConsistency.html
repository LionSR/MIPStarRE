    Projective consistency transport #

    Compatibility barrel for the projective-consistency part of the mainFormal assembly, covering the paper's ζ₃ = 6ζ₁ + 6ζ₂ step (\Cref{eq:third-goal}). The underlying declarations are split into four sub-leaves:

    • ProjectiveConsistency.Evaluation — data-processing lemmas converting polynomial-level projective consistency to pointwise consistency after evaluation at a sampled point (consRel_constPolynomialEvaluation).

    • ProjectiveConsistency.Handoff — projective handoff residuals packaging the line-156 (ζ₃) projectivization handoff together with the two evaluated line-172 links.

    • ProjectiveConsistency.EvaluationHandoff — intermediate residual recording the polynomial-level line-169 ζ₁ consistency and the data-processed line-172 pointwise links.

    • ProjectiveConsistency.CompletionTransport — the finer completion-transport residual that reconstructs the line-156 handoff from completion closeness and transports the repaired line-169 consistency estimates to the two final point-consistency targets at ζ₄.
