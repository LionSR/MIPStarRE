    Section 5 — restriction of completed projective submeasurements #

    This file contains the elementary order algebra used after applying the orthonormalization theorem to the option completion of a submeasurement. If the repaired projective family keeps at least the original residual mass at the fresh none outcome, then the total of the retained some outcomes is bounded by the original submeasurement total.

The source submeasurement has a single outcome of mass zero, while the completed projective submeasurement has the identity on the original outcome and zero on the fresh outcome.
