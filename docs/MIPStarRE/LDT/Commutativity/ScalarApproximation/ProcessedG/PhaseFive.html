    Phase 5 stability defect infrastructure #

    Internal helper definitions and lemmas for the Phase 5 scalar bridge. These definitions extract and bound the one-dimensional stability defect controlled by gCommStabilityTwo_scalar (the boundedness part of the paper's clm:g-comm-stability2), reindex the question-level defect, and perform the subtraction algebra.
