    Section 11 commutativity: evaluated-slice averaged expansion #

    Averaged evaluated-slice expansion of qSDDOp into the four projector terms BAB + ABA - BABA - ABAB, used to reduce the commutation to per-projector estimates.

    • arXiv:2009.12982, Section 11 (commutativity of the Pauli-X and Z players).
