    Opens and Over categories #

    In this file, given a topological space X, and U : Opens X, we show that the category Over U (whose objects are the V : Opens X equipped with a morphism V ⟶ U) is equivalent to the category Opens U. This equivalence is bi-continuous, and thus induces an equivalence of sheaf categories.

    source
    def TopologicalSpace.Opens.overEquivalence {X : Type u} [TopologicalSpace X] (U : Opens X) :
    CategoryTheory.Over U ≌ Opens ↥U

    If X is a topological space and U : Opens X, then the category Over U is equivalent to Opens ↥U.
