    The bounded below derived category #

    Let C be an abelian category. In this file, we show that the bounded below derived category DerivedCategory.Plus C (defined as a full subcategory of DerivedCategory C) is the localization of the bounded below homotopy category HomotopyCategory.Plus C with respect to quasi-isomorphisms.
